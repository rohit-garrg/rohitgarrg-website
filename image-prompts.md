# Image Generation Prompts for Writing Section

Style notes for all images:
- **Ethnicity:** All human subjects should be visibly Indian (South Asian features, skin tones)
- **Setting:** Modern Indian urban context — Delhi NCR corporate offices, expressways, Gurgaon/Noida apartments
- **Clothing:** Business casual for office scenes, casual for home/commute scenes
- **Age:** Primary subject appears to be a professional in early-to-mid 30s
- **Quality:** Photorealistic unless otherwise noted
- **Avoid:** Generic Western stock photo aesthetics, overly staged poses, exaggerated expressions
